About Me

  • I am a PI and Assistant Professor at Greehey Children's Cancer Research Institute, UT Health San Antonio
  • I am a computational biologist. My research is focused on cancer genomics and bioinformatics. 
  • I am a CPRIT Scholar in Cancer Research

My Training Background

  • My undergraduate training was in Biochemistry at Nanjing University, China. 
  • I received my PhD in Bioinformatics from Shanghai Institutes for Biological Sciences. 
  • During my postdoctoral training, I worked in TCGA consortium in Dr. Roel Verhaak's lab. 
  • I also spent a little more than 1 year at Vanderbilt as a postdoctoral fellow.

My Research Interest 

  • I am primarily interested in finding patterns in the cancer genome. 
  • I work on childhood cancers, adult cancers, and cancer models. 
  • I am enthusiastic about mining of public datasets. 
  • I am also interested in aging and new computational methods.
