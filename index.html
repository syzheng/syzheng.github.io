About Me (first name: Siyuan, last name: Zheng) (郑思远)

  • I am a PI and Assistant Professor at Greehey Children's Cancer Research Institute, UT Health San Antonio [11/2017-]
  • I am a computational biologist. My research is focused on cancer genomics and bioinformatics. 
  • I am a CPRIT Scholar in Cancer Research

My Training Background

  • My undergraduate training was in Biochemistry at Nanjing University, China [09/2000-06/2004] 
  • I received my PhD in Bioinformatics from Shanghai Institutes for Biological Sciences, China [07/2004-06/2009] 
  • I recieved training on cancer genomics at MD Anderson Cancer Center [05/2011-10/2007] 
  • I also spent a little more than 1 year at Vanderbilt University as a postdoctoral fellow [09/2009-04/2011]

My Research Interest 

  • I am primarily interested in finding patterns in the cancer genome. 
  • I work on childhood cancers, adult cancers, and cancer models. 
  • I am enthusiastic about mining public datasets. 
  • I am also interested in aging and new computational methods.
